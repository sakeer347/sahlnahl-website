From QHSE Manager to Sustainability Entrepreneur

I didn't start my career in sustainability‚ÄîI started in operations. For 7 years, I worked as a QHSE Professional, from Fire Watcher to Aviation QHSE Manager, across FMCG, aviation, and construction industries. I implemented ISO 14001 environmental management systems. I conducted energy audits. I trained teams on waste reduction.

But I kept seeing the same pattern: companies wanted to improve their environmental performance, but they didn't know how. Or they'd hire expensive consultants who'd deliver beautiful PowerPoint strategies that never got implemented because they didn't understand operational realities.

"Most sustainability consultants come from academic backgrounds. I come from operations. I've spent 7 years on factory floors, construction sites, and aviation facilities implementing environmental management systems. I know what actually works in real companies."

That's when I realized: the market is ready. Abu Dhabi's new regulations are creating urgent demand. Mid-market companies need solutions NOW.

So here we are. A consultancy built on operational experience, not academic theory. Focused on mid-market affordability, not Big 4 prestige pricing. Committed to implementation, not just strategy.
